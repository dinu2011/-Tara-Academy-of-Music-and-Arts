    Hello all! I am a funny, friendly, fun-loving person with deep Indian roots with a plethora of hobbies! I love to paint, sing Indian classical music, play the violin. When it comes to painting, I have tried different kinds of paintings including glass painting, acrylic painting, traditional Kerala Mural! I have also been learning the violin since childhood and my expertise is in Carnatic Music. I play different Kritis, Kannada Bajans and also love to practice exercises like Alankaram, Varnam, etc., Recently, I have ventured my skills in singing and I am learning various kritis and Kannada devotional sings! I strongly believe these are an essential part of rejuvenation in my life and greatly help me in bursting my stress!

    Professionally, I am a Mechanical Engineer that graduated from a college based in Chennai and went on to pursue a Master’s from the University of Windsor, Canada. I completely believe and admire sustainable energy given the fact that there are growing concerns about climatic change. I strongly welcome the Indian and Canadian Government’s commitment to reduce greenhouse gas emission, however, I still feel we have a long way to go. As a small contribution to start with, I work as a project manager in a solar company based in Toronto and I feel complacent to note that I am helping people go green and play their role in saving the planet!

    This combination has given me confidence in my skills while leaving me the constant reminder that I am never done learning and can continually improve, as well as develop new techniques.

    Please feel free to contact me with any project, comment or thought you might have!s
